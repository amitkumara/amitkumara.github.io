About

Hello, I am the third year PhD student at GREYC lab, Université de Caen (France), under the supervision of Prof. Marc Spaniol. I am the part of HULTECH team. My main research interests are Natural Language Processing (NLP), Web Science and Information Retrieval. Currently, I am exploiting entity-level analytics for the semantic contextualization of the Web contents.

Broad Research Interests: NLP, Web Science, Information Retrieval
Current/Specific Interests: Entity-level Analytics, Semantic Analysis of Documents
